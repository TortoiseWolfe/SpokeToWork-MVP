## Contact Script (Phone)

> "Hi, I'm Jonathan Pohlner, a Cleveland developer. I'm building a hiring tool that helps employers find candidates who can reliably get to work — specifically for workers without cars. Looking for local employers to pilot. Could I speak with someone in HR about the transportation challenges you see with entry-level hires?"

---

## Discovery Questions

1. How often do new hires miss shifts or quit due to transportation?
2. What percentage of applicants don't have reliable cars?
3. Do you have trouble filling weekend/evening shifts specifically?
4. Have you tried anything to help employees with transportation?
5. Would you pay for a tool that reduced no-shows?
